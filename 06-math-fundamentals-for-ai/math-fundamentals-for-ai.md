## Combinatorics
### Binomial coeficient
**N above R**:
- number combinations of *n* elements taken in groups of *r* elements
- number of subsets of *r* elements that has a set of *n* elements

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
n\\
r
\end{pmatrix}
=
\dfrac{n!}{r!(n-r)!}
$$
